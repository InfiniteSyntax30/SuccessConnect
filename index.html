ABOUT US

Success Connect International(SCI) was established in 2020 by CEO Taiwo Afolalu with the purpose of providing trainings in computer-related fields and programmes. Our trainings are for everyone; pupils, students, corp members, working adults; literally everyone that has an interest in acquiring a skill in the field of computer studies. SCI is a 100% hands-on practical training institute where you learn by doing.

We are here to empower you to be more comfortable with this type of technology, from learning the parts of a computer to using a mouse, to using the various programs and packages on a computer. We offer outstanding training in Microsoft Applications such as Word, Excel, Access, PowerPoint, as well as other popular applications such as CorelDraw and other graphic applications. We offer introductory level, for novices, thorough advanced level courses, for seasoned PC users. All our courses offer top quality hands-on instruction at our comfortable learning environment. In today's high demand for professionals in this field, we need to compete more efficiently to keep up with the demands required in business. Success Connect International Computer Institute offers practical computer skills training for you to incorporate into your daily life quickly and effectively. Contact us to find out how these courses can leverage your abilities to a higher level of performance.
